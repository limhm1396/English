[현재] - I do it.
1. 현재의 상태
I am a student.
I am tired.
I am in my room.
I love chocolate.
I have a car.
I know your name.
*상태동사를 현재시제로 쓰면 주어의 현재 상태를 표현한다.

2. 습관, 경향, 직업
I eat breakfast.(습관)
Babies cry all the time.(경향)
I teach English.(직업)
*동작동사의 현재형은 주어의 습관, 경향, 직업을 나타낸다.

3. 진리, 과학적 사실, 속담
The earth is round.
The earth moves around the sun.
Water boils at 100'c.
Old habits die hard.
Once bit, twice shy.

4. 이미 예정된 일
My EBS lecture comes on Sunday at 8 am.
This program airs every Sunday.
*이미 정해진 스케줄은 현재시제로 미래를 나타낼 수 있다.
The performance starts at 7 o'clock.
The bus leaves at 8:30 in the morning.
*왕래발착동사도 현재형으로 미래를 대신해서 쓸 수 있다.

5. 현장감을 표현할 때
He opens the window and feels the wind.
She throws the noodle in the pot and stirs it with chopsticks.
He kicks off his back leg and stretches his front one to support the throw.

[현재진행] - I am doing it.
V -> be V-ing = ~하는 중이다.
I am running.
I am studying.
I am cooking.
*지금 당장하고 있는 거라면 현재진행형을 쓰면 된다.
*상태동사는 따로 진행형을 만들어 쓰지 않는다.
I am thinking.
*동사의 의미를 강조하기 위해 상태동사를 현재진행형으로 쓰는 예외적인 경우도 있다.

1. 지금 당장 일어나고 있는 행위
It's raining outside.
He is washing the dishes.

2. 습관적으로 자주 일어나는 행위
You are always eating snacks before dinner.
She is always making the same grammar mistakes.

I am studing in visual design.
I am writing a book.
*직업/전공 등을 현재진행형으로 표현하기도 한다.

3. 가까운 미래
I am moving to Seoul soon!
I am coming!


[현재완료] - I have done it.
V -> has/have PP
과거에 시작된 일이 현재까지 영향을 미치는 경우.
과거부터 시작된 일이 현재 시점까지 이어져서 현재시점에 완료된다.
= ~했다.

1. 결과적 용법
과거에 일어난 일의 결과가 현재에도 영향을 미칠 때 사용
= ~해본 적 있다.
I've lost my cell phone.
I've lost three kilograms.
You've become my best friend.

2. 경험적 용법
과거부터 지금까지 한번이라도 겪은 적 있는 특정 경험
Have you been to Europe? / I only have been to France twice.
Have you seen that movie? / No, I haven't! Is it good?
Have ypu met Ted? / Yes, I have. Hi Ted!

3. 완료적 용법
현재와 매우 가까운 과거에 완료된 일을 표현
= 방금 막 ~했다.
I've just finished reading a book.
I have already paid off all my loan.

4. 계속적 용법
과거에 일어난 일이 현재까지 지속되고 있는 경우
= 지금까지 ~해왔다.
I've studied English since I was 17.
We have been together for five years now.

[현재완료진행] - I have been doing it.
V -> have been V-ing
= 지금까지 ~하는 중이다.
I have been studying English for 11 days with Banana teacher.

[과거] - I did it.
V -> V-ed

1. 과거에 있었던 일
I had lunch before.
I finished my homework last night.
I met her yesterday.

2. 역사적인 사실
King Sejong the Great created Hangul.
On August 15 in 1945, Korea was liverated.

used to V: (규칙적으로) ~했었다.
I used to go to church.

would V: (불규칙적으로) ~했었다.
I would go to church.

[과거진행] - I was doing it.
V -> was/were V-ing
과거의 한 시점에 진행 중이었던 일
I was working out.

[과거완료] - I had done it.
과거 = V-ed / 더 과거 = had PP
A and B had started their relationship in 2019 but they broke up in 2020.

1. 결과적 용법
더 과거에 있었던 일의 결과가 과거 시점까지 영향을 줄 때
When he came to see me, he had dyed his hair.
I had lost my cell phone when he called me.

2. 경험적 용법
더 과거에 일어난 일의 결과가 가까운 과거까지 영향을 끼칠 때
I had never had a girfriend until 40.
I was the only one who had had kangaroo meat.

3. 완료적 용법
과거와 매우 가까운 과거에 완료된 일을 표현할 때
When I wanted to get back with him, he had already moved on.
Public transport had stopped running so I took a taxi.

4. 계속적 용법
더 과거에 일어난 일이 특정 과거 시점까지 지속되었을 때
They had been together for 5 years.
She had not seen him after they broke up.

5. 대과거
한 문장에서 과거보다 더 과거에 일어난 일을 나타내고 싶을 떄
The car had been his proudest possession before he got a new one.
I got back home after he had left.

[과거완료진행] - I had been doing it.
had been V-ing
= 그때까지 ~하는 중이었다. / 그때까지 ~해오고 있었다.
The company had been making plans for new products.
We had been getting along so well.

[미래] - I will do it.
will + V / be going to + V = ~할 것이다.

1. be going to + V
이미 시작되었거나 확실한 계획이 있는 미래
I am going to see him now.
I am going to eat a lot tonight.
I am going to meet John tonight.
I am going to be 'the Photographer of the Year'.

I am not going to change my mind.
She is not going to come back.

I am gonna give you a lesson.
He is gonna be with his wife.

2. will + V
be going to가 확신에 찬 미래라면 will은 주어의 희망이 담긴 미래
I will call you later.(조금 거리감이 있는 미래에 일어날 일)
If you should be in trouble, I will be there for you.(미래의 일에 대한 의지나 결심을 표현)
It will rain tomorrow.(자연현상/시간의 흐름에 의해 당연히 일어날 미래의 일)
I will be 30 soon.
He will be there, right?(미래에 대한 막연한 추측)

3. 왕래발착동사로 미래를 표현
I am starting my own company.
He arrives at 10 am.

4. be to 미래 용법
= ~할 예정이다.
Arty and Banana are to attend the Christmas party.
Wendy and Steve are to be married on October 10th.

5. be about to V
= 지금 막 ~하려고 하다.
I am about to go get some water.
She is about ro cry.

[미래진행] - I will be doing it.
will be V-ing = ~하는 중일 것이다.
미래의 특정 시점에 하고 있을 일을 예측해서 쓰는 시제
I will be studing for my final exam this weekend.

[미래완료] - I will have done it.
will have PP = ~쯤이면 ~한 상태일 것이다.
과거, 현재, 미래에 일어난 일이 미래의 어느 시점에 완료된 상태일 것을 예상해서 쓰는 시제
I will have finished my final exam by then.
After one year, you will have forgotten all of your French.
At the end of the trip, you will have become a new person.
By the time you get up, we will have gone.

[미래완료진행] - I will have been doing it.
will have been V-ing = ~해오는 중일 것이다.
I will have been living on the street for 10 years next month.
