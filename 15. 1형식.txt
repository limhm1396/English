[1형식의 특징]
주어 + 동사 + 부사/전명구 = 주어가 동사하다.
*부사와 전명구는 넣고 빼는게 자유롭기 때문에 모든 형식의 문장을 자유롭게 드나들 수 있다.

- be
I am in my office.
I am on the top of the mountain.
God is in my heart.
*be동사는 주로 전명구랑 함께 쓰인다.
- go
Let's go for a walk.
- come
Winter is comming.
- depart
The flight departs soon.
- happen
What happened?
- appear
Crack appeared in the walls.
- show up
He won't show up on time.
- disappear
The problem won't just disappear.
- stand
Don't stand outside in the cold.
- walk
I walked around the city.
- run
Water runs through the fiter.
- fly
Birds fly in the sky.
- sweat
I sweat like a pig.
- exercise
I exercise every day.
- fit
These shoes fit perfectly.
- weight
Air weights.
- stay
I stayed up all night.
- do
Anyone will do.
*1형식 do는 '충분하다'로 해석한다.
- count
Your appearance doesn't count in a job interview.
*1형식 count는 '중요하다'로 해석한다.
- pay
This job pays well
*목적어가 없는 1형식에서 '수지가 맞다'로 해석한다.
- read
This book reads well.
*보통 타동사라고 생각하는 단어가 1형식 자동사로 쓰일 때는 수동형으로 해석되기도 한다.
- sell
This car sells the most

[특이한 1형식 형태]
There is/are + S = 주어가 있다.
*주어보다 동사가 먼저오는 구조
*there은 해석하지 않는 유도부사
There is an elevator in my apartment.
A dog is -> There is a dog.
People are -> There are people.
There is a dog in front of my school.
There are people in the house.
