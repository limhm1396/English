[분사]
a.k.a.동형사

[현재분사]
V-ing: ~하는

[과거분사]
PP: ~된, ~진, ~받은

[분사의 역할]
1. 명사를 꾸미는 분사(한정적 용법)
I want to write a moving story.
She gets a fixed income from her company.
Joseph Biden was Obama's running mate.
No one ever died of a broken heart.
He gave me a book written in French.

2. 보어 역할을 하는 분사(서술적 용법)
- 2형식 주격보어
I was moved by his movie.
The book was very disappointing.
His speech was so boring.
Everyone got bored.

- 5형식 목적격보어
Leave the door closed.
I heard my music playing in a recode shop.
I saw her dancing.
Keep your mouth shut.

3. 동사의 시제와 수동태
- 진행형
be V-ing: ~하는 중이다.
- 완료형
have PP: 지금까지 ~해왔다.
- 수동태
be PP: ~되다, 지다, 받다

[분사의 형태와 해석]
1. 기본 분사
- V-ing: ~하는, ~하는 중인
sing -> singing
clean -> cleaning

- PP: ~된, ~진, ~받은
check -> checked
make -> made

2. 복합분사
- 부사 + V-ing: ~하게 ~하는
hard-working employee
fast-running man
early-rising sun

- 부사 + PP: ~하게 ~된
well-made album
newly-married couple
newly-born baby
well-known YouTuber

- 형용사 + V-ing: ~한 상태로 ~하는
good-looking man
delicious-looking food

- 형용사 + PP: ~한 상태로 ~된
hard-boiled egg
ready-made sauce

- 명사 + V-ing: ~를 ~하는
time-consuming work
english-speaking people
video-making companys

- 명사 + PP: ~에 의해 ~된
hand-make car
sun-dried tomato
home-cooked meal

3. the + 분사
- the V-ing: ~하는 사람/물건
the dying, the dancing
- the + PP: ~된 사람/물건
the dead, the unexpected

4. 유사분사
분사는 아니지만 분사와 유사한 역할을 하는 형용사
형용사 + 명사-ed: ~한 명사를 가진
hot-tempered people
brown-eyed girls
warm-hearted person

[감정분사]
현재분사는 주어가 어떠한 감정을 주는 상황
과거분사는 주어가 어떠한 감정을 느끼는 상황

I heard shocking news yesterday.
The class was so boring.

You disappointed me.
-> You are very disappointing.
-> I am disappointed in you.

You are embarrassing!
-> I am embarrassed of you!
-> You embarrassed me!

[분사의 준동사적 특징]
1. 동사적 의미를 가진다.
2. 동사의 짝을 받는다.
I am very satisfied with your performance.
There is a baby sleeping in my room.
3. 부정을 나타낼 수 있다.
